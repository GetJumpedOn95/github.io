Description of ISP

The goal of our ISP is to create a scheduling device that can help students at Allen High school choose their classes for next year.We want to provide an easy and intuitive way of making the selection of classes go smoothly. We will do this by using an easy to read UI and making everything students need accessible and easy to find.This ensures that students can register and select the classes they want for next year without any difficulties selecting the classes of their choice.
